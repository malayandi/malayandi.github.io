  • Bio:
  • I am a junior at the University of California, Berkeley, majoring in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science. I also go by the name Malayandi Palaniappan (mainly on formal documentation) or Andi Palaniappan.

  • My interests, broadly speaking, lie in pure mathematics, artificial intelligence (my area of research) and teaching.

  • My current research is focused on developing robust approaches to the value alignment problem. I work under the supervision of Anca Dragan and Stuart Russell at the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research Lab (BAIR).

  • I have taught at Berkeley as a Undergraduate Student Instructor (UGSI) for three semesters, for courses in probability theory, linear algebra, differential equations.

  • I co-founded and help run the Palan Foundation, an educational non-profit based in Malaysia. We hope to improve the accessiblity of education to students from disadvantaged communities in Malaysia.
