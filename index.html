  • News:
  • This semester (Spring '17), I am serving as an Undergraduate Student Instructor (UGSI) for Ani Adhikari's offering of Stat 134 (Concepts of Probability).

  • As a result, I have left my teaching position at the SLC after three wonderful semesters. I consider myself extremely fortunate to have had the privilege of working there and am extremely grateful for the friendship and guidance offered by all the staff.

  • Bio:
  • I am a junior at the University of California, Berkeley, majoring in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science. I also go by the name Malayandi Palaniappan (mainly on formal documentation).

  • My academic interests lay primarily in artificial intelligence and machine learning, and more loosely in probability and numerical analysis.

  • I have been teaching at UC Berkeley in various capacities for four semesters now, for courses in probability and linera algebra.

  • Recently, I co-founded an educational non-profit in Malaysia, the Palan Foundation, which I now currently help run.
