  • Andy Palan (also known as Malayandi Palaniappan) is a student at the University of California, Berkeley, majoring in Applied Mathematics and Statistics.

  • I have been teaching mathematics and statistics at UC Berkeley's Student Learning Center (SLC) for the last three semesters. This coming semester, I will be teaching the SLC's support lecture for Stat 134 (Probability).

  • My academic interests lay primarily in probability and linear algebra as well as in their intersection with Computer Science (namely, in the fields of machine learning and numerical analysis).
