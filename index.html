  • This summer, I will be continuing work on my research in the field of Collaborative Inverse Reinforcement Learning at Berkeley.

  • Bio:
  • I am a junior at the University of California, Berkeley, majoring in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science. I also go by the name Malayandi Palaniappan (mainly on formal documentation).

  • I am currently serving as an Undergraduate Student Instructor (UGSI) for Ani Adhikari's offering of Stat 134 (Concepts of Probability). Click here to go to the companion sites for my sections of this course.

  • I am deeply passionate about education and have been teaching at UC Berkeley in various capacities for four semesters now, primarily for courses in probability and linear algebra.

  • My academic interests lay in the rather broad field of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML), but particularly in the field of Inverse Reinforcement Learning. I am currently working under the supervision of Dylan Hadfield-Menell and Stuart Russell at Berkeley's Center for Human Compatible AI (CHCAI), with my friend and fellow undergraduate researcher, Dhruv Malik

  • Recently, I co-founded an educational non-profit in Malaysia, the Palan Foundation, which I now currently help run.
